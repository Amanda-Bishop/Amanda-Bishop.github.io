WCA Summer Camp

Who What When Why

Who

Girls from grades 5-8 who are interested in learning programming that will allow you to create a basic website.

An Example

What

A five day long camp that will teach young girls how to build a basic website and learn some engineering through crafts and have fun!

When

From July 9th to the 13th from 9am to 3pm.

Why

Why Code Aurora was created last year when my sister and I realized how large the gender gap is in technology. We found out that at the University of Waterloo their first year Computer Science and Computer Engineering classes only have 20% women and this was a large number compared to other universities. Since we feel that girls are just as capable as boys of going into a technology field we wanted to change this. We ran an event in October where women in technology spoke to the girls in out community about their experiences as a woman in tech and encouraging these young girls to get interested in technology. After the event I still felt that there was more I could do in my community to get girls interested in technology, so I came up with the idea for this summer camp. The girls do not need to have any prior knowledge about technology because this course is meant for beginners.
