Hi SHAD Committee,
For my Creative Demonstration for my SHAD 2019 Application, I made this website. I decided to make a website because I recently learned HTML and CSS and I love to improve my skills by building websites. What I love about technology is that you can problem solve, it takes creativity, and when you hit an obstacle you have to persist to find a solution. In this website, I put my extracurriculars and some of my favourite things. To me, technology means making creative and unique solutions to the world's problems and I can't wait to be a part of this field one day.
