Why

Why Code Aurora was created a while ago when my sister and I realized just how large the gender gap is in technology. We learned that at most universities their first-year Computer Science and Computer Engineering classes only have approx. 20% women. Since we feel that girls are just as capable as boys of going into a technology field, we wanted to change this. We ran an event in October 2017 where women in technology spoke to the girls in our community about their experiences as a woman in tech. They encouraged these young girls to get interested in technology. After the event, I still felt that there was more that I could do in my community to get girls interested in technology. I came up with the idea for this summer camp. The girls do not need to have any prior knowledge about technology because this course is meant for beginners.
